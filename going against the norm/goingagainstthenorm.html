Going Against The Norm

When Piero Fornasetti was creating these multiple portaits of Lina, also known as his work "The Lady", he stated in an interview: "Something beautiful can be duplicated a million times and still be just as beautiful.

As he began to make a variety of different versions of "The Lady", he used different mediums to create his prints. He used paint, pen,charcoal, and develped these designs as prints, plates, furniture, etc. This furthered his expansion of "The Lady" and changed the rhetoric that something repeated is "boring".
