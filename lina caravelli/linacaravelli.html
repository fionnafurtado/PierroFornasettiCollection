Lina Caravelli was an Italian opera singer and actress. She was born in Italy and lived most of her adult life in Paris. She was once deemed the "Most beautiful woman in Paris" during the 1920's.

When Fornasetti came into contact with Lina, he immeditatley felt a sense of inspiration. He went on to create "The Lady" which would lead to him being known for this piece.
